About

What is Chibi Link?

Chibi Link is a simple and efficient tool designed to make sharing long URLs easier and cleaner. Our service takes lengthy links and compresses them into short, customizable URLs that are perfect for social media, messaging, and other platforms.

Why Use Chibi Link?

Chibi Link makes URL sharing simple and secure. Create short, easy-to-share links instantly, perfect for social media and messaging. Each link is unique and protected, ensuring safe and efficient sharing every time.

How It Works

Simply paste a long URL into our input field, click on the "Scissors", and instantly receive a short, easy-to-share link. You can use Chibi Link to save time and effort while sharing important links with friends, colleagues, or your audience.
